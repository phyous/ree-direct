Ree.direct The simplest way to remember and share links

Remembering long URLs is impossible. Ree.direct lets you link any url to an easy to remember keyword of your choice.

Ex: ree.direct/gangnam can point to https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=9bZkp7q19f0
